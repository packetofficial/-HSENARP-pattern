MARKING SCHEME


1. Theory Examination - 100 marks

2. Internal Assessment - 100 marks

1. Theory Examination- In theory Examination a question paper is divided into two sections i.e SECTION A - 40 marks , SECTION B - 60 marks .

Section A ( ALL QUESTIONS ARE COMPULSORY )

QUESTION 1 [5 Questions of 2 Marks each = 10 Marks]

QUESTION 2 [5 Questions of 2 Marks each = 10 Marks]

QUESTION 3 [10 Questions of 2 Marks each = 20 Marks]

Section B consists of total 6 question of 15 each , in which we have to attempt only 4 of our choice .

2. Internal Assessment

*Attendance

*Practical Works

*Assignment

*Practical Record

*Class Work Note Book

*Performance of the student in the class



» Assignments - 40 Marks

» Practical Work - 40 Marks

» Class room Performance - 20 Marks
